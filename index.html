Huckle Buckle Beanstalk

The game 'Huckle Buckle Beanstalk' is commonly known as 'Hot or Cold' game.

On start of the game, a randomly generated number is selected by the game between 0 and 100. The player will then attempt to guess the number selected by the game in as little tries as possible. I know what you are thinking, the odds of guessing the correct number is a hundred to one, but don't worry, we have you covered. On each guess you make, there will the a response telling you how close your guess is to the number. A response 'Hot' means you are close, a response 'Cold' means your guess is far from the number and the response 'Warm' means you are somwhere in between.

Now that you have what you need to play, go ahead and see if you can guess the number!!!
